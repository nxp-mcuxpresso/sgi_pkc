--- New Features
   - Fixed Copyright Headers (The only change in the DCI_SDK_V0.2.1) 
   - The support of Diffie-Hellman Key Agreement over Finite Fields (FFDH).
   - The fixes of Coverity/MISRA/MSG violations
   - The hardenings (The Security Counter based Flow Protection)

--- Known Issues/Limitations:
   - To use any Asymmetric Cryptographic Operations the RAM_INTERLEAVE flag in SYSCON must be enabled before invoking any CLib Asymmetric Cryptographic Functionality.
   - The PRNG after Power-On Cycle/System Reset has seeded with 0 hence will output the deterministic behaviour. Will be fixed in future release
   - Not all Coverity/MISRA/MSG violations fixed. The work in progress and will be finished in future release.
   - Not all Hardenings yet finished. Will be finished in future release.
   - Limited testing (all examples passed)
     - ECC and RSA Key Generation test with RNG patching are failing.
     - Test framework warnings
